Filing Information

What to File

The Uniform Commercial Code ("UCC") establishes a standard for recording liens across the country. The Secretary of State processes liens which protect financial interests on, for example, consumer goods or commercial equipment.

Each document receives a unique 12-digit filing number assigned by our UCC program, and is available for viewing on our UCC search as public record.

  • UCC-1: Financing Statement — creates a lien filing in the system
  • UCC-1a: Financing Statement Addendum — additional debtor, secured party, and collateral fields
  • UCC-1ap: Additional Party — provides for multiple additional debtors and secured parties
  • UCC-3: Amendment — amends previously filed liens
  • UCC-5: Information Statement — allows debtor or secured party to dispute a filing
  • UCC-11: Search Request — provides copies, certified copies, or debtor listings
Filing Fees
  • UCC-1: $9.00
  • UCC-1a / UCC-1ap: No fee (must be submitted with UCC-1)
  • UCC-3: $9.00 for continuations, assignments, and amendments; $2.00 for terminations only
  • UCC-5: $9.00
  • UCC-11: Varies by search requested

Filing Length: Standard liens are effective for 5 years. Tax, Restitution, Racketeering, and Utility liens do not expire.

Applicants agree to pay a $25.00 fee if their account returns insufficient funds.

Filing Tips
  • Our office does not require acknowledgment copies.
  • Certified UCC search results cannot be acquired on our public search website. All filings will be watermarked "uncertified".
  • If you have questions, call us at 602-542-6187 or 1-800-458-5842.
